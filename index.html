Hussain's Legacy

Hussain Ibn Ali (AS) was Ali Ibn Abi Talib (AS) and Fatimah (AS)’s Younger son
Hussain was born on 3 Sha’aban (AH 4) in Medinah, the city of Saudi Arabia
Grandson of Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W) and Khadija (AS)
After the Death of Hasan (AS) [Elder brother of Hussain and Elder son of Ali], Hussain (AS) kept the peace as per treaty between Hasan (AS) and Muawiyah
Imam Hussain (AS) never accepted Yazid, as the successor after the death of Muawiyah. The reason behind not accepting Yazid as a caliph was Umayyads were oppressive and religiously extremist and misguided people
People of Kufa sent letters to him asking for his Help. (Which was a plan to invite him and martyr him
Hussain (AS) did not take any kind of army with him to Karbala
On his way to Karbala, he encountered with the Army of Ubaydullah ibn Ziyad. Who Hussain (AS) commanded that people of Kufa invited him as the people needed an IMAM
Two days away from Kufa, Hussain (AS) along with his family the people of Kufa opposed his claims. At a point near Euphrates river, Hussain (AS) and his family stopped by Yazid’s army and restricted their reach to get the water.
After reaching Karbala, Imam Hussain (AS) knew that it will be his final resting place. About Imam Hussain, (AS) martyrdom in Karbala was already been told even before his birth!
No Army Imam Hussain (AS) didn’t take any Army with him to Karbala (because he was there to make peace, not war!) There were only 72 people with Imam Hussain (AS), including Woman, Children, and his Family members because he was there to make peace, not war
Returning of Hur to Imam Hussain (AS)Hur, who was in the army of Yazid, came back to him and then joined him. Hur was the person who stopped Imam Hussain (AS) and his family members to get the water! Hur then came back and asked for forgiveness which Imam Hussain (AS) accepted and forgave him
Ali Asghar (AS)” was the youngest Martyred of KarbalaA poisoned arrow struck into his name when he was just 6 years old, He was the youngest person to be martyred in the battle of Karbala.
The only male survivor in the battle of Karbala was Zain Ul Abideen (AS) Zain Ul Adibeen (AS) was ill and due to his illness, he didn’t fight. After the martyrdom of Imam Hussain (RA), he was kept as prisoners among with other women and children left in Imam Hussain (AS)’s family
The last martyr in the battle of Karbala was Imam Hussain (AS) ALLAH HO AKBER! martyred Imam Hussain (AS) by cutting off his head, Imam Hussain (AS) was the last martyr in this battle.
